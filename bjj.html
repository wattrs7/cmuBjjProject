Brazillian Jiu Jitsu

Intro To Brazillian Jiu Jitsu

Brazillian Jiu Jitus or BJJ is a martial art that focuses on grappling. As such, the core techniques of BJJ involve taking an opponent off of thier feet, controlling them on the ground using pins, and ultimately submitting them using joint locks or strangulation techniques.
